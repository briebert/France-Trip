⛵Venice Transport
Venice has no cars — everything is on foot or by vaporetto (water bus). Buy a 24-hr (€25) or 48-hr (€35) pass rather than single tickets. Line 1 travels the Grand Canal. Line 4.1/4.2 → Murano. Line 12 → Burano. Rolling luggage is difficult — cobblestones, canal bridges, narrow alleys. Pack soft-sided bags for Venice if possible. Airport transfer: Alilaguna Blue Line (~70 min, €9) or ATVO express bus (~20 min, €10) from Piazzale Roma.
